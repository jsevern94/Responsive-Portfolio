About Me

To date, I have worked in various industries, from service, hospitality and tourism, to customer support, sales, and online marketing. I have some background in html, css, javascript and other front and back end technologies, as well as some experience with WordPress. I am frequently coming up with new business ideas and plan to finally start to implement them now that I am learning the skills to build webpages.

In my free time, I like to read, compose and play music, play video games, and exercise. My interests range from geography, culture, and science to psychology, philosophy, and even spirituality. I'm very social, and love to meet new people, especially when games or lively discussion are involved.

I also enjoy traveling and have spent several years living abroad. I have visited 16 countries in Europe and I plan to spend some time in Asia and Oceania next.
